Data Deletion Request

If you want to delete your data related to our app, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to muhammad.shakir@bic.edu.pk with the subject: Data Deletion Request.
  2. In the email, include your Facebook User ID and a request to delete all associated data.
  3. We will process your request within 24-48 hours and confirm via email once the data is deleted.

Thank you for using our app!
